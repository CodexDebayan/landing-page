Our vision.

Grow and Glow is a student organization under the aegis of Department of Student Organization, Division of Youth Affairs, Student Welfare(wing) in Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, Punjab. We bring you opportunities that are worth it for you to stage your talents. The entire purpose of a student organization is to give environment of co- operate sector as is the need of our, and make students to be creative and have their own identity by listening to their inner voice.And its also made for students goals.

Here you have an opportunity to learn about yourself, your strengths and weaknesses, your goals in life, and more. Your are in between students of your own age who will teach you different things and you can learn how to handle certain situations from them. There is an opportunity for you to find what you actually enjoy, how you multitask, stay focused and organized severe others and even generate ideas. sint!

Message from our Founder and CEO

Mr. Akash Yadav (Founder)

We began with the broad idea of using the prosperity we had been blessed with to aid those less fortunate than ourselves. However, we soon saw the need for a more focused approach in our philanthropic work. Today’s world has an endless number of compelling social issues, and without a concentrated effort, our impact would be diluted. We needed a clear focus in order to get the most out of our resources.
